  • About Me

    For Everyone:

    Firstly, I quit my job at the beginning of 2017 to find something new. In the time since then, I have built a company with two of my friends from college. That company is StoragePug! The self storage industry is my newest love, and I plan and hope to stay here for a long time.

    Second, I'd like to think I'm good at spending the little free time that I have. I've toured with my band, Lions, all around the country and I love hiking // camping // backpacking. Other things I love include riding my motorcycle, disc golf, and chasing sunsets.



    For Engineers:

    I am a full-stack web developer with a focus on back-end. I've worked in both solo and team development settings. My recent projects have been built in Symfony and Angular, but I'm always open to new frameworks and believe that the technology should suit the project. I enjoy learning new languages, frameworks, and technologies. In addition to the Web, I've written system applications for image processing and AI.



    About this Site:

    I built this site in pure javascript (i.e. no jQuery) with the help of npm, grunt, and bower. The frameworks used were hover.css, sequence.js, pure.css, sass, spin.js, and tingle.js. For the server side of the contact form, I wrote a little PHP script.
